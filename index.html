Who we are

At Events By CK, we believe that every event tells a unique story. We are a team of passionate, creative, and dedicated event planners committed to turning your visions into unforgettable experiences. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we specialize in crafting events that are personalized, seamless, and extraordinary.

With a keen eye for detail and a knack for innovation, we handle every aspect of the planning process—ensuring that your event is not only beautiful but also executed flawlessly. Whether it’s a wedding, corporate function, or private party, our goal is to bring your dream event to life while you enjoy the moment.

We are dedicated to delivering exceptional event planning services, and creating unforgettable experiences for various celebrations. Let us take care of the details so you can focus on what truly matters—celebrating life’s most important moments.

Our services

We offer a full range of event planning services tailored to meet your unique needs. From concept development and venue selection to décor design and day-of coordination, we handle every detail to ensure your event is a success. Our expertise includes weddings, corporate events, baby showers, and many more special occasions of all sizes. We work closely with trusted vendors, providing custom solutions that match your vision and budget.
